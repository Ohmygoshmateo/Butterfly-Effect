# The Butterfly Effect 🦋

**Decision Path Modeling; one decision, three honest futures.**

The Butterfly Effect is a browser-based web application that helps people think through major life decisions. Describe a decision in plain language — quitting a job, enlisting, going back to school, moving, starting a business — and the application shows you a realistic **best case**, **most likely case**, and **worst case** across three time horizons, plus the other areas of your life the decision tends to affect.

It never gives advice. It shows you the landscape honestly; the decision stays yours.

**Live site:** `http://butterflyeffect.asia/` *(updated to refelct new website domain)*

---

## Features

- **Three honest paths** — best case, most likely, and worst case for every decision, mapped across near term, mid term, and long term horizons
- **Eight life domains** — career, military, business, education, finance, health, relationships, and location, detected automatically from your wording
- **216 hand-written outcome narratives** — every run selects different variants, so re-running the same decision tells the story a genuinely different way
- **Personalization** — optionally add your age and situation (family, savings, priorities) and the paths adjust to fit your life
- **Ripple effects** — see what else the decision touches: money, family, health, network, and more
- **Timeline & Snapshot views** — read outcomes in time order, or compare all horizons side by side for one path type
- **100% private** — no account, no installation, nothing you type is stored or sent anywhere; works offline once loaded

## Built With

- **HTML / CSS / JavaScript** no frameworks, no libraries, no external dependencies
- No APIs or AI services at runtime the outcome engine is a self contained keyword classifier and curated content library
- Hosted free on **GitHub Pages**

## Running It Locally

1. Download all five files into one folder:
   `index.html`, `butterfly-effect.css`, `bg-hero.jpg`, `bg-mist.jpg`, `matthew-photo.jpg`
2. Double-click `index.html` — it opens in your browser, ready to use
3. No internet connection, account, or setup required

## Disclaimer

The paths shown by this application are realistic generalizations based on how similar decisions tend to unfold. They are **not predictions and not advice**. For decisions involving significant financial, legal, medical, or emotional matters, use this tool as a starting point and consult a qualified professional.
